Bethenny Frankel's Skinny Margarita

Mention it all, MENTION IT ALL!

Description

Have you ever wondered how to make Bethenny Frankel's Skinny Girl Margarita? Well look no further! The natural food chef—nope, not a cook, KKB—and self-made business mogul put herself on the map with her twist on the classic margarita by making it skinny. Below is Frankel's recipe for her famous Skinny Girl Margarita. Sorry, Lu, no creds for you here. You'll get your moment in the Eggs a la Francaise recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 2 ounces of clear premium tequila
  • Splash of citrus liqeur (Grand Marnier, Quantro, Triple Sec)
  • Tiny splash of club soda for light weights

Steps

Instructions per Bethenny Frankel on fncimag.com

  1. Roll lime on cooking surface to release juices
  2. Cut lime in half and squeeze into shaker
  3. Pour a 4-count of tequila in shaker
  4. Add splash of citrus liqeur of choice. Ensure it's a little splash to avoid the additional sugar
  5. Give it a shake and pour in margarita glass
  6. Add one more squeeze of lime wedge. Here's your chance to add a tiny splash of club sode if desired
  7. Enjoy your margarita while feeling like a Skinny Girl!
