Magic Tagliatelle

Eat It and Enjoy It -- Live La Bella Vita and Look Great, Too!

Description

Here we have the ingrediences for Teresa Giudice's Magic Tagliatelle! Any Italian from New Jersey knows their food, and Teresa is one of them. But don't be fooled by the rich and decadent Italian cuisine, as this recipe comes from Giudice's Skinny Italian cookbook. You'll want to flip some tables after eating this dish! And don't forget the sprinkle cookies for dessert!!

Ingredients(ingrediences)

  • 1 lb. linguine or tagliatelle pasta
  • 3 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 6 oz. thick-sliced ham, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 cup fresh cooked or thawed frozen peas
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • Parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. (Try to time it so your pasta and sauce are done at the same time.) Make sure to set aside 1 cup of the cooking water. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil over medium heat in a medium-sized skillet. Add onion and garlic and cook 3-5 minutes until soft. Add the butter and melt it down.
  3. Add the peas and the ham and cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to low to keep warm.
  4. Drain the pasta and return to the pot. Add the sauce, salt, and pepper. Toss the pasta and add enough of the cooking water to make a light sauce.
  5. Top with parsely
